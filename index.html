Background

Based in Milan, born and raised in Mexico City, I bring over five years of experience working across fashion styling, visual research, and content production. My work focuses on developing strong visual narratives that translate brand identity into compelling imagery and digital content.

Alongside traditional creative processes, I actively experiment with AI tools to support concept development, moodboarding, and visual storytelling.

This portfolio presents selected projects and creative explorations that reflect my approach to fashion image-making and brand communication.
